making an account on civitaz

Want to use our publicly available xmpp server for your own private and secure communication? Great. Here is how.

You need to use an xmpp client to sign up currently. I will be working on bringing web registration to this site soon.

  • Download an xmpp client - we're recommending a few at this link that you can check out. They are free and open source software, just like xmpp.
  • On your xmpp client, find "create an account" or the equivalent, and create your account using either "examplename@civitaz.xyz" and your desired password, or "examplename" as your username and "civitaz.xyz" as the domain - depending on the xmpp client. Replace examplename with the username of your choice.
  • Once your account is created, sign in using the details you specified.

Once you are successfully signed into your new xmpp account on civitaz, you can add people from any other xmpp server. All you need is their username and server name (yours is yourusername@civitaz.xyz).

We will be making some short how-to's on how beginners can sign in and register with some of the best xmpp clients.

Keep your eye out for a new page here soon - I aim to bring web registration to make it that much simpler to sign up and begin chatting.
