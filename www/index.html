civitaz.xyz - free public xmpp server

This is civitaz. It is an xmpp server and the future home of some other federated projects.

civitaz xmpp

The civitaz xmpp server is open for public registration. You can also register on almost any XMPP client on every platform.

Web registraion is now open!

Click on the new web registration button below to register your account here on the site. You can also click on civitaz web portal to access civitaz online without a client.

We use converse.js, a free and open-source plugin, to provide our web portal for civitaz.
