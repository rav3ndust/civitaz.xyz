civitaz.xyz

This is civitaz. It is an xmpp server and the future home of some other federated projects.

civitaz xmpp

The civitaz xmpp is open for public registration. At the moment, it requires an xmpp client to create your account.

We will, however, be building in a way to register accounts right here on the site.

===how to register an account===

  • Download an xmpp client - we're recommending a few further down the page that you can check out. They are free and open source software, just like xmpp.
  • On your xmpp client, find "create an account" or the equivalent, and create your account using either "examplename@civitaz.xyz" and your desired password, or "examplename" as your username and "civitaz.xyz" as the domain - depending on the xmpp client. Replace examplename with the username of your choice.
  • Once your account is created, sign in using the details you specified.

Once you are successfully signed into your new xmpp account on civitaz, you can add people from any other xmpp server. All you need is their username and server name (yours is yourusername@civitaz.xyz).

We will be making some short how-to's on how beginners can sign in and register with some of the best xmpp clients.

Gajim - Linux

This is a great xmpp client for Linux distributions that comes packaged in most distro's standard repositories.

It is lightweight, classic-looking, and extensible with PGP and OMEMO encryption.

To get it, just run:

sudo apt install gajim

in Debian/Ubuntu based distros, or:

sudo pacman -Sy gajim

For Arch based distros.
